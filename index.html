FAQ's

Plus One

Due to limited space, we are only able to accommodate those guests formally invited on our wedding invitation. We want to be surrounded by our families, friends, familiar faces, meaning we can only take those who are invited. Please refer to the number of seats allotted for you. Thank you for your understanding.

Kids

We love children, but we cannot accommodate them at the venue. This is an adults-only affair. The only kids attending are those who are part of the entourage and those only requested. We hope you understand.

Parking

Parking space is available in the vicinity. It is inside our venue. Kindly seek assistance from traffic enforcement personnel for guidance.

Arrival Timing

The ceremony begins promptly at 3:00 PM, with the entourage walk starting at 2:30 PM. Please plan to arrive at 2:00 PM to allow ample time for parking and finding your seat.

Leave Time

Please do not eat and run. It took us months to plan our wedding. Stay with us until the end of the program. If you must leave early, please personally come to thank us and bid goodbye before you go, perhaps not until our same day edit video is played.

Photo/Video

We want photos and videos captured from our day, especially from our friends’ and families’ point of view. Meanwhile, during the ceremony, we kindly ask that you refrain from taking photos and videos as we want to savor the solemnity of the moment and honor God as we receive His blessing.

RSVP

Unfortunately, if we do not receive your RSVP by the specified date, you will not be able to attend. If you were to show up without submitting an RSVP, there would be no seat for you. If you need to update your RSVP, simply send us a message.

Pets

Even if we love pets so much, we are sorry to inform you that the ceremony venue does not allow any pets in the area, and we also want to keep the same vibe and solemnity during the reception, so we are hoping for your understanding about this.

We hope to see you from the altar.

We would be delighted to celebrate with you. Kindly respond through our RSVP form.

Cheers!
